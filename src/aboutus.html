Policarpio State University


History

Policarpio State University was founded in 2004 with the vision of providing accessible and quality education to students in the community. At its beginning, the university offered only a few academic programs and served a small number of students. Over the years, it expanded its courses to include education, business, agriculture, engineering, arts and sciences, and other fields to meet the growing needs of society. Since its establishment, Policarpio State University has remained committed to academic excellence, research, and community service. From its humble start in 2004, it has grown into a recognized institution that continues to shape competent, responsible, and service-oriented graduates who contribute to national and global development.

Our Mission

"To provide foster critical thinking, nurture talents, and instill integrity and social responsibility to become competent professionals and responsible citizens of our nation.”

Our Vision

“To inspire and empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will help them succeed in their careers and contribute positively to society.”
